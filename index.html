Frequently Asked Questions

What if my kids won't cooperate?
This happens more often than you'd think — and it's totally fine. We're experts at working with children of all ages. The candid, in-between moments often become the most treasured images.
Can we include pets?
Absolutely! Pets are family. Just let us know in advance so we can plan the session flow accordingly.
What's the best time of day?
We strongly recommend golden hour — about 1-2 hours before sunset. The light is warm, soft, and magical, and it makes every family look their best.
Is the 11x14 print included automatically?
Yes! After gallery delivery, we'll reach out to help you select your favorite image for your complimentary 11x14 archival print.

Wherever your story takes you

Some of the most breathtaking images happen far from home — on cliff edges in Santorini, in lavender fields in Provence, on quiet beaches in Tulum, or in the heart of a city you've always dreamed of visiting.

Our Destination Session is perfect for elopements, anniversary getaways, honeymoon portraits, or any moment you want to commemorate somewhere extraordinary. We collaborate with you on every detail of the itinerary to maximize light and location.

Destination Session Gallery

Frequently Asked Questions

How are travel costs calculated?
Travel fees cover flights, accommodations, and ground transport at cost. We're transparent about all travel expenses before you commit to booking.
Can this be used for an elopement?
Absolutely — many of our destination sessions are intimate elopements. We help coordinate timing around permits, golden hour, and ceremony logistics.
Do you know good local spots?
We've shot across Europe, the Caribbean, Mexico, and throughout the US. For new destinations, we research thoroughly and often partner with local guides.
What if the weather doesn't cooperate?
We always build a backup plan. Moody weather often produces the most dramatic images — and we'll work with whatever conditions we're given to create something extraordinary.

Bridal Portrait Session

Starting at $750
← Back to Services

Your wedding day moves fast.
This doesn't.

A Bridal Portrait Session is two unhurried hours dedicated entirely to you — your gown, your beauty, your story. Before the vows. Before the chaos. Before the day belongs to everyone else.

This is the session where we slow down, breathe, and create images that are purely and completely about you. No timeline pressure. No groom waiting. No wedding coordinator rushing us. Just you, your gown, and the kind of light that makes everything feel like a dream.

The result is a collection of fine art portraits that stand entirely apart from your wedding day gallery — images of you at your most radiant, preserved with the same care and artistry that earned recognition at the Portrait Masters Awards.

What's Included
  • 2-hour studio session exclusively for the bride
  • Professional studio lighting and backdrop setup
  • 20–40 hand-edited digital images
  • Private watermarked online gallery
  • Full print release included
  • Fine art print options available
  • Styling consultation provided

Book Your Bridal Session

Limited dates available. Secure yours before your wedding day.

Package Price Images
Essential $750 20 images
Signature $950 30 images + 11x14 print
Heirloom $1,200 40 images + album + 2 prints

Bridal Portrait Gallery

Two hours. One bride. Infinite elegance.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I schedule my Bridal Portrait Session?
Ideally 2–4 weeks before your wedding day. This gives us time to deliver your gallery before the wedding, and you'll be completely comfortable in front of the camera by the time your wedding day arrives.
Do I wear my actual wedding gown?
Yes — and it's one of the best reasons to do this session. You get to wear your gown in a relaxed environment, work out any movement or comfort concerns, and have stunning portraits in it without the pressure of your wedding day timeline.
Can I do multiple looks or gowns?
Absolutely. Within the 2-hour session we can accommodate 1–2 gown changes depending on styling complexity. Let us know during your consultation and we'll plan accordingly.
Is hair and makeup included?
Hair and makeup are not included but we have a list of preferred South Florida beauty artists we love working with and can connect you with. Many brides use this session as a hair and makeup trial run — a smart way to test your wedding day look.
