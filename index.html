Frequently Asked Questions

When should I schedule my Bridal Portrait Session?
Ideally 2–4 weeks before your wedding day. This gives us time to deliver your gallery before the wedding, and you'll be completely comfortable in front of the camera by the time your wedding day arrives.
Do I wear my actual wedding gown?
Yes — and it's one of the best reasons to do this session. You get to wear your gown in a relaxed environment, work out any movement or comfort concerns, and have stunning portraits in it without the pressure of your wedding day timeline.
Can I do multiple looks or gowns?
Absolutely. Within the 2-hour session we can accommodate 1–2 gown changes depending on styling complexity. Let us know during your consultation and we'll plan accordingly.
Is hair and makeup included?
Hair and makeup are not included but we have a list of preferred South Florida beauty artists we love working with and can connect you with. Many brides use this session as a hair and makeup trial run — a smart way to test your wedding day look.
